HAIRCUT


At Perry's Barber Studio, we understand that a perfect haircut is an important key to confidence. Our premier services achieve it. You’ll find that our high-end studio has little in common with an old-fashioned barbershop other than personalized attention and outstanding quality. We represent a fresh, modern, and premier list of services to reveal and maintain the ideal you.

HOT TOWEL SHAVES


While shaving can be synonymous with razor burns, cuts, nicks, and skin irritations, hot towel shaves actually open the pores of the skin and soothe. The steam from the hot towel allows dirt, excessive oils, and dead skin to be more easily removed. The treatment softens and relaxes the hair, improves the health of the skin, and protects against burning and itching.

HOT TOWEL FACIALS


Our effective process has an immediate and big impact on facial tissues, pores, and muscles. Opening up the pores promotes the release of stored toxins while also exfoliating the skin to eliminate dead cells and make way for new ones. We further promote circulation by getting the blood moving, relieve stress, and lessen muscle tension.

BEARD SERVICES


An attractive beard requires care. When it’s trimmed into shape, with the strays removed, your beard compliments your face. Dark Horse Barber Studio provides a comprehensive list of beard services to provide a well-maintained, well-defined, and flawless-looking beard. Let us help you determine and achieve your best look with a thorough consultation, removal of excess length, and precision shaping.

About us

Truly authentic and traditional barber experience

Perry's barber shop is a truly authentic and traditional barber experience. Nowadays many men are returning to old-fashioned traditions to get a special shave ritual and they’re finding the comprehensive attention they need at the new brand barbershop that mix the past and present. The aim for this branding was to capture the hand aesthetic of the craft, while capturing the original atmosphere of vintage barbershops with a part of old seaside legends.
